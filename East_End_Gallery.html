About Us

East End Gallery was set up under the banner ‘East Eurpoe Art Gallery’ in 2007 and has since continued to promote and exhibit talented artists from all over Eastern Europe. With the intention of making ‘Art Affordable for Everybody’ and with the introduction of the new generation into the family business, the gallery chose to rebrand itself.

Vision:
- Showcase paintings and artworks of young European artist's.
- Introduce clients to the best of Indigenous European art.

Artist

Natalie Dyer, the founder of East End Gallery, is the major driving force of the gallery. Her passion for art and business can be gauged from the fact that almost all the aspects of the gallery from communicating with artists, sales, online promotions, exhibition planning, etc. are looked after by her. Her educational background of Masters in Arts Management from Birkbeck College, University of London, UK and practical experience gained by working with various art galleries in UK and India, has helped him understand the art business and develop a passion for it.
